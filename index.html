Longer pendulums swing more slowly.

Approximate \(g \approx 9.8\,\text{m/s}^2\) on Earth.
Damping
Energy loss per swing (air resistance, friction).
Animation speed

Click and drag the bob to choose the initial angle.

Running
Period:
State
Math
Exact equation
\[ \theta'' + \frac{b}{m}\,\theta' + \frac{g}{L}\sin\theta = 0 \]
We integrate the full nonlinear equation numerically.
Small-angle approximation
\(T \approx 2\pi\sqrt{L/g}\)
For \(|\theta| \lesssim 10^\circ\), \(\sin\theta \approx \theta\) and motion is SHM.
